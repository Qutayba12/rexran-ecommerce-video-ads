Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rexran ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you visit rexran.com or place an order for our creative services.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us, or that is necessary to deliver our service:

  • Order & contact details: your name, email address, brand or store name, product links, any offer you'd like featured, and the notes you submit through our order or contact forms.
  • Payment information: when you pay, card details are entered directly into our payment processor (Stripe) and are never stored on our servers.
  • Basic technical data: standard information your browser sends, such as device type, used only to keep the site working and secure.

How we use your information

  • To produce and deliver the creative work you order.
  • To contact you about your project, timeline, and delivery.
  • To process your payment securely.
  • To respond to questions you send us.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

Service providers we use

To run our business we rely on a few trusted third parties, each with their own privacy safeguards:

  • Stripe — to process payments securely.
  • Vercel — to host the website.
  • Zoho Mail — for our business email.
  • Soda Marketing AI — to produce the creative deliverables.

These providers only receive the information needed to perform their function.

Data retention

We keep your order and contact information only as long as needed to deliver your project and meet legal or accounting obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at hello@rexran.com and we'll respond promptly.

Cookies

Rexran does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any storage used is strictly necessary for the site to function.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, reach us at hello@rexran.com.
