The Studio

Ads directed by hand. Produced with AI.

Great ads used to mean a studio, a crew, and a two-week wait. Rexran was built to break that trade-off.

Most small brands are stuck choosing between an agency they can't afford and DIY creative that looks like everyone else's. Rexran exists to end that compromise — pairing an art director's eye with AI production, so a store scaling on a tight budget can run creative that looks like it came from a studio.

Every project is directed by hand, one brand at a time. No templates, no prompt-and-pray, no assembly line. Just ads built to earn attention and move product — delivered in days, not weeks.

AI-directed, not AI-generated

There's a real difference between typing a prompt and directing a shoot. AI is the camera and the crew — it handles the actor, the voice, the render. But every script, cast, shot, and color grade is chosen and reviewed by a person, project by project. Nothing goes out that wasn't directed on purpose. That's why the result reads like a brand's ad, not a generic AI clip.
